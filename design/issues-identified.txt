APPROACH TO FIXING

All HTML pages were updated to include proper charset and viewport meta
tags. Navigation nesting was corrected across all four pages to use li
elements wrapping anchor tags. Nav hrefs were updated to relative paths
for local file compatibility. DOCTYPE declaration was made consistent
across all pages.

The contact form was rebuilt with 5 input types, the text, email, textarea,
radio, and checkbox with correct label associations and required,
minlength, and maxlength validation attributes. The table caption was
fixed by removing the invalid h3 nesting. Heading hierarchy was corrected
by consolidating the photo section under the background article.
Descriptive alt text was added to all images.

For CSS, the stylesheet was expanded to use 5+ selector types including
element, class, ID, descendant, and pseudo-class. Hover, focus, active,
and nth-child pseudo-classes were added throughout. Navigation, table,
and form styles were completed. Border, margin, and padding properties
were applied consistently across all elements. The lightblue hero
background was replaced to fix the contrast issue. The footer was
centre-aligned. Meaningful comments were added throughout explaining
each section of the stylesheet.
