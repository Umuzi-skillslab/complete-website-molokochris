My Background

I’m Chris, a South African developer and designer passionate about building practical, impactful digital products. I specialise in web and mobile development using modern technologies like React, React Native, and Next.js, with a strong focus on performance, usability, and clean design.

My journey into development started with a curiosity for how things work, which grew into a commitment to creating solutions that solve real-world problems. I’ve worked on a range of projects—from mobile apps that leverage smartphone sensors, to platforms that connect local businesses with customers.

Currently, I’m focused on building products that empower people and communities. One of my key projects aims to help users discover and support local businesses more easily, while also giving entrepreneurs a platform to grow their visibility. I enjoy working across the full stack, using tools like FastAPI for backend development and Firebase for scalable, cost-efficient infrastructure.

I’m particularly interested in building systems that are efficient, accessible, and scalable from day one. Beyond coding, I have a strong interest in product design and user experience. I believe that great software isn’t just functional—it should feel intuitive and seamless to use.

My long-term goal is to grow my work into a company that builds meaningful digital solutions, while continuing to learn, experiment, and improve with every project.
